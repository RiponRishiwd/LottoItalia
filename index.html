Lottery Italia

The Lottery Italy, the only Lottery currently in force at national level, is in the collective imagination an event with a strong symbolic and cultural value. The edition 2024, starting on September 16, 2024, ends on January 6, 2025.

Starting from September 16th, players from all over Italy have the opportunity to participate in the draws of the daily prizes announced from October 6th to December 27th, from Sunday to Friday, during the broadcast of the combined RAI television program “Your business ” and the January 6 draw of the Italian Lottery 2024 prizes. The first category prizes will also be announced during the broadcast “Affari tua ”, which will air on January 6, 2025 in the early evening
