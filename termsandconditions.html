Legal basis and purpose of processing

The legal basis is contained in the relevant national legislation - Law 722/1955; Law 62/1990; Legislative Decree 78/2009, converted into Law 102/2009 - which reserved the management and operation of national lotteries with deferred draws to the Customs and Monopolies Agency.

The processing of personal data is aimed at managing the Lotteria Italia 2024 - referred to in Directorial Determination no. 570626/RU of 13 September 2024 - and in particular to:

allow the purchase of tickets in digital format through the Lotterie Differite Telematiche platform;
allow participation in the draw of daily prizes awarded and communicated during the television broadcast “Affari Tuoi”;
authorize the payment of prizes.
Type of data processed
To participate in the Lotteria Italia 2024 it is not necessary to provide any personal data with the exception of personal and financial data for the sole purpose of allowing the payment of any prizes awarded, in compliance with the provisions in force.

There are some services for which the provision of common personal data is optional, but without which it is not possible to access. In particular:

The request to purchase digital tickets involves the need to provide some data (game account identifier);
Participation in the daily prize draw involves the need to provide some data (telephone number).
Methods of processing
The Customs and Monopolies Agency ensures that the processing takes place for the sole purposes indicated above and in any case adopts adequate procedures and suitable measures to guarantee the confidentiality of the winner's identity at all times.

Personal data are processed with automated and non-automated tools, for the time strictly necessary to achieve the purposes for which they were collected. The Customs and Monopolies Agency adopts appropriate security measures, including organizational, technical and physical ones, to ensure that personal data are processed appropriately, in accordance with the purposes for which they are managed and to prevent their disclosure, modification, destruction, and unauthorized consultation. The data provided may be transmitted to subjects whose communication must be carried out in compliance with an obligation established by law, by a regulation or by EU legislation, or to comply with an order of the Judicial Authority.

Data recipients

The personal data provided, if necessary, may be communicated:

to subjects to whom the communication of the data must be carried out in compliance with an obligation established by law, by a regulation or by EU legislation, or to comply with an order of the Judicial Authority;
to subjects designated by the Data Controller, as Processors, or to persons authorized to process personal data who operate under the direct authority of the Data Controller or the Processor;
to other possible third parties, in the cases and/or under the conditions established by national and Union law, or to public authorities, in particular if the communication is necessary for the protection of the Agency in court, in compliance with the current provisions on the protection of personal data. Exercise of rights
The interested parties may obtain, from the Data Controller, confirmation of the existence or otherwise of the processing of personal data and exercise, where permitted, the rights referred to in Articles 15 to 21 of the GDPR. In particular, it is possible to exercise the right of access, rectification of inaccurate personal data and to obtain the integration of incomplete data.

The limitation and opposition to the processing as well as the deletion of the necessary personal data provided, prevent participation in the Lottery and/or the payment of any prizes.

Further information regarding your rights on the protection of personal data can be found on the website of the Guarantor for the Protection of Personal Data at www.garanteprivacy.it;

If the interested party believes that the processing of personal data has occurred in violation of the provisions of the GDPR, he/she has the right to lodge a complaint with the Supervisory Authority, as provided for by art. 77 of the Regulation itself at www.garanteprivacy.it;.

Data Controller

The data controller of personal data is the Agenzia delle Dogane e dei Monopoli, with headquarters in Rome, Piazza Mastai, 12 – 00153; e-mail:
dir.internalaudit.datipersonali@adm.gov.it; to whom the interested party can contact to exercise the rights indicated above.
